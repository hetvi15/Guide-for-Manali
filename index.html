Valley Of Lords

Insanely popular tourist destination,Manali also called Valley of Lords,offers a widely exotic and spiritually mesmerizing panoramic view of Himalayas and the fast bounding river Beas in the foreground.

History

The name Manali is regarded as the derivative of 'Manu-Alaya' which literally means 'the abode of Manu'. Legend has it that sage Manu stepped off his ark in Manali to recreate human life after a great flood had deluged the world.

Attractions

On a trip to Manali, tourists must visit the Great Himalayan National Park, Hadimba Temple, Solang Valley and tiny lake of the Beas Kund and Rohtang Pass. The Pandoh Dam, Chandrakhani Pass are other prominent attractions in the city. Truly heaven and beyond.

Treks

Owing to the high altitude and mountainous terrain, trekking in Manali plays an important role in making it a famous tourist destination. The town acts as a base and commencement point of several famous hikes, which take tourists deep into the Himalayas.

Adventures

Manali is the hub of adventure activities in Himachal Pradesh. From white river rafting to zorbing to horse-riding to skiing to paragliding, Manali has everything to offer.

Accomodations

With millions of options in Valleys of lord also there is a wide range of luxurious, mid-range, budget hotels, homestays, villas and resorts here.
