5 Best Places to Visit Manali

1. Hidimba Temple

Hidimba Devi temple is one of the best visits of ManaliIt is located in the middle of Dhungari temple, in the canopy of Pine Trees.It is an ancient cave temple dedicated to Hidimbi devi,wife of Bhima,son of Pandu,in the epic Mahabharata.Festivals sucha as Dhungari Mela take place there.It was built in 1553.The main attraction is the scenic beauty of the temple.

2. Rohtang Pass

The vista point of Rohtang Pass is the ultimate and almost essential attraction of the itinerary. Located at a distance of just 51 kilometres from the main town, Rohtang Pass can be reached only by road. Rohtang pass is an experience of nature in one of its rawest forms possible, amongst mountain peaks covered with snow, breathtaking view and magnificent and sprawling landscapes and views. The magnificence of the white landscape and sonorous silence is a mesmerizing thing worth experiencing.

3. Solang Valley

When heading for Manali Solang valley is also one of the essential visit.From sports activities like paraglidingand zorbing it also offers great aesthetic pleasure due to the beautiful greenery and white covers during winters. It is one of the most famous place for trekking and adventures.People love to spend time here to relax and to rejuvenate.

4. Vashisht Hot Water Springs

Vashisht Temple is famous for its natural hot water spring. The premises enclose a pyramid-shaped stone temple dedicated to Sage Vashisht, and the water spring, which is said to have medicinal properties, are both one of the famous places to visit in Manali. Take a dip in the water to relax your body and mind. One of the most spiritual and the best healing experiences of the life,one should not miss this oppurtunity.

5. Jogini Falls

Jogini Falls is situated on the Northern edge of Vashisht Village . The waterfall cascades down from a height of around 150 feet through multiple levels. There is a small pool at the base formed by the fall and visitors can take a plunge in the water and spend some time. Jogini mata temple is at the base of the waterfall. The trek to Jogini Falls is among the best short treks near Manali. The trek route goes through apple orchards, tall pine trees and small water streams. The whole trek route is very scenic through beautiful villages aligned with the sprawling valleys. This is one of the best short treks in Manali to enjoy local scenic beauty and village life in Himalayas. At the highest point,due to te angular beauty rainbows are also seen.It is the sight to behold and cherish forever.
